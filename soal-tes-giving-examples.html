36. 3. Give 5 examples of giving personal point of view​


Personal Point of View

We use these words and phrases to express a personal point of view:

“In my experience…”
“As far as I’m concerned…”
“I’m absolutely convinced…”
“Speaking for myself…”
“In my opinion…”
“Personally, I think…”
“I’d say that…”
“I’d suggest that…”
“I’d like to point out that…”
“I believe that…”
“What I mean is…”
“It’s obvious to me…”

EXAMPLE :
1. I’m absolutely convinced that the best way to improve your English is to live in an English-speaking country such as the US.
2. It’s obvious to me that the best way to improve your English is by using English, not studying English.
3. As far as I understand it, you need to practice on a regular basis if you want to improve your English.
4. It seems to me that there is no best way to learn English. Each person has to find a strategy or method that works for them.
5. In my experience, watching and listening to films and TV in English can really help.
