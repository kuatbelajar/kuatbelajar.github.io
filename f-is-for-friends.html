jawaban :

maaf kalau salah

Jawaban:

Adilla made some cookies for her friends.

Penjelasan:

Simple Past Tense

Kita lihat Interrogative Form-nya, ada kata did di awal kalimat sehingga kita bisa mengetahui bahwa kegiatan tersebut terjadi di masa lalu atau past. Untuk dijadikan Postitive Form, kita hapus did lalu mengubah verb make menjadi made.

Rumus Simple Past Tense Interrogative Form: Did + S + V1 + O

Rumus Simple Past Tense Positive Form: S + V2 + O

Penjelasan:

Pernyataan yang salah diatas memiliki arti Arya tidak marah kepada temannya karena membicarakannya juga tidak marah dengan mereka karena merahasiakan sesuatu dibelakangnya

*For Your Information : neither...nor artinya tidak mendukung, sedangkan either...or artinya mendukung

23. contoh: to ask for my friends attention           to check if my friends understand           to give my opinions and ask for my friends opinions           show my appreciation to my friends


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: english
Kategori: various expression
Pembahasan:

Example for
asking for attention:
- Attention, please
- Guys, listen up, would you?
- Silence, lads. Let's hear what he'll say.

check for understanding:
- Do you understand it?
- Do we understand each other?
- Do you know what I mean?
- Got it?

Give opinion and ask about it:
- I agree with you (+)
- I couldn't agree more (+)
- I disagree with you (-)
- I'm afraid I don't share the same opinion with you (-)
- I don't think I can see your point (-)
- What do you think?
- What's your opinion about...?
- What do you say about...

Showing appreciation:
- Well done, mate!
- Good job, kids!
- That's a nice work, man!
- Great work, dude! I don't think I can do what you did there.
