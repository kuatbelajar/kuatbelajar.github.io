Mention the connectors of cause and effect in the paragraph 5. Mention passive sentence in the paragraph!​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

The paragraph is about the rule prohibiting students from chewing gum in the class and the reasons for this rule.

"First, some irresponsible students make messes with their gum" and "Another reason why I don't allow students to chew gum is because it is a distraction" are the cause sentences in the paragraph.

"They may leave it on the bottoms of desks, drop in on the floor, or put it on other people's property" and "When they are allowed to chew gum, students are more worried about having it, popping it, chewing it, and snapping it that they are in learning" are the effect sentences in the paragraph.

"because" and "is" are the connectors of cause and effect in the paragraph.

"are not allowed" is passive sentence in the paragraph.
