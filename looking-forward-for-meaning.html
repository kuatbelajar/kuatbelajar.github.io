1. "i'm looking forward to getting your reply soon" looking forward has the similar meaning to.... a. waiting for b. seekong for c. looking at d. looking after e. looking for


Jawaban:

A. Waiting for

Berdasarkan arti:

"i'm looking forward to getting your reply soon"

= "Saya menantikan balasanmu segera"

looking forward, has the similar meaning to....

= looking forward, memiliki arti yang mirip dengan ....

a. waiting for = menunggu ✅

b. seeking for = mencari

c. looking at = melihat ke

d. looking after = merawat

e. looking for = mencari

Kesimpulan:

• yg ditanyakan adalah similar meaning (arti yg mirip)

✅ looking forward = menanti

✅ waiting for = menunggu

• maka Jawaban adalah A karena arti keduanya MIRIP yaitu sama MENANTI/MENUNGGU

Jawaban dicetak tebal pilihan A. looking forward to dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Phrasal Verbs (frasa kata kerja) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

11. Shilpi _________ a suitable match for her dressA. is looked forB. is look forC. is looking ford. are looking for​


Jawaban:

C. is looking for

Penjelasan:

rumus present continuous tense

(+) S+ to be (am/is/are) + V-ing + O

(-) S+ to be (am/is/are) + not + V-ing + O

(?) to be(am/is/are) + S + V-ing + O

jadi

Shilpi is looking for a suitable match for her dress

21. What is the similar meaning of ‘looking forward’? *a. Waitingb. Seekingc. Readingd. Applyinge. Asking​


Jawaban:

B. Seeking

Penjelasan:

'looking forward' : sedang mencari

a. Waiting : menunggu

b. Seeking : mencari/ pencarian

c. Reading : membaca

d. Applying : menerapkan

e. Asking : meminta

JAWABAN :

Waiting (A)

PENJELASAN :

'Looking forward' sama artinya dengan 'Waiting', yaitu 'Menunggu'. Jadi, jawabannya Waiting (A)

Jadi kalo : 'I am looking forward to hearing from you soon' berarti artinya : 'Aku menunggu kabar darimu segera'
