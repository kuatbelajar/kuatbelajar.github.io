Make sentences into a dialogue using Present Perfect Tense

Dialogue 1 - Seeing an exhibition

Maya: Have you seen the arts exhibition at the local museum?Bobby: No, I haven't. When did it start?Maya: Yesterday, and it will end the day after tomorrow.Bobby: Then I should hurry. I love arts, so I certainly don't want to miss it. Thanks for letting me know, Maya.Maya: You're welcome, Bob.

Dialogue 2 - Joining a contest

Candra: Hi, Tina. You enjoy singing, don't you?Tina: You know it well yourself. Why?Candra: Have you ever thought of joining a singing contest? You have a chance now. There is a Youth Singing Contest this Saturday at the mall.Tina: I do enjoy singing, but before the crowds? I'm not sure. How do I sign up?Candra: It's easy. You can simply go to the mall now and register yourself at the information booth.Tina: Well, I will have to think about it. Thanks for the information Candra.Candra: Anytime, Tina.

Dialogue 3 - Having an unforgettable moment

Hafiz: Patricia, have you been to Bali before?Patricia: Certainly, and I'll never forget about it.Hafiz: Is Bali such a great place?Patricia: Absolutely. I went there 2 years ago, and it was a surprise from my father. At first we had wanted to go to Kepulauan Seribu, but my father changed his mind and made a new arrangement secretly. I'm glad that he did.Hafiz: I hope I will have the chance to visit Bali myself.Patricia: You certainly will, and I promise you that you will enjoy it as much as I have.Hafiz: Thanks. I hope so too.

24. MATERI (Congratulations AND Wish)SOAL Andi join english speech contest​


Jawaban:

Congratulations and Wish:

Budi: Congratulations on winning our school's speech contest, Andi!

Andi: Thank you, Budi! I wished you could join the speech contest as well.

Budi: Yeah, I wished I could! I am Happy that you won the contest.

Andi: That makes the two of us. Thanks for your support, Budi!

Budi: My pleasure!

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Conversations (percakapan) tentang Congratulaing Someone (mengucapkan selamat kepada seseorang) dan Wish (mengharapkan) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Percakapan:

Budi: Selamat atas kemenangan lomba pidato sekolah kita, Andi!

Andi: Terima kasih, Budi! Aku berharap dirimu bisa bergabung dengan kontes pidato juga.

Budi: Ya, aku berharap aku bisa! Aku senang kamu memenangkan kontes.

Andi: Itu membuat kita berdua (merasa senang). Terima kasih atas dukunganmu, Budi!

Budi: Dengan senang hati!

Situasinya adalah Andi memenangkan kompetisi pidato sekolah dan sebenarnya Andi berharap bahwa saat pertandingan / kompetisi / kontes pidato tersebut terlaksana (di masa lampau), Budi juga ikut menjadi salah satu peserta kompetisi, namun kenyataannya karena satu dan lain hal Budi tidak ikut berkompetisi dalam kontes pidato tersebut.

Congratulating Someone:

Congratulations + on + Verb-ing + Object

Wish:

(+) Subject + Wish(ed) + Subject + Could/Would + Verb-1
