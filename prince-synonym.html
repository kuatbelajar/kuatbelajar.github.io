Question and answer :

What is the synonym of had?

Synonyms of had are possessed, owned, acquired, obtained, got, held, etc.

----------

Had adalah konjugasi dari kata kerja have yang berarti memiliki/mendapat.

Had adalah bentuk past participle  (v) dari have.

------------

Contoh kalimat yang menggunakan kata had dan sinonimnya.

Tom had an A for his English test.

Tom got an A for his English test.

Mr Smith had an old car.

Mr Smith owned an old car.

Mr Smith possessed an old car.

He had a key in his hand.

He held a key in his hand.

I had the painting at an auction.

I obtained the painting at an auction.

I acquired the painting at an auction.

Reinalfi dan prince sedang berlatih voli,reinalfi memukul bola bermassa 0,4 kg dengan kecepatan awal 4 m/s ke arah prince,prince kemudian menangkis bola sehingga bola bergerak dgn kecepatan 3 m/s ke arah budi,jika waktu sentuh bola saat di tangkis prince sebesar 0,01 s,hitung gaya implus yg di hasilkan prince


Mata pelajaran : Fisika
Kelas: X SMA
Kategori : momentum dan impuls
Kata kunci : perubahan momentum, impuls

Kode kategori berdasarkan kurikulum KTSP : 11.6.6

Diketahui
• m = 0,4 kg
• v = 4 m/s
• v' = 3 m/s
• Δt = 0,01 s
Ditanya
• I = ?

✐ jawab
I = Δp
I = p' - p
I = m.v' - m.v
I = m (v' - v)
I = 0,4 (3 - 4)
I = 0,4 x -1
I = -0,4

Maka, impuls / perubahan momentum yang dihasilkan sebesar -0,4 Ns
