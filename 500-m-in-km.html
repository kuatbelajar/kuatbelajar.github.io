Penjelasan:

Berikut adalah konversi satuan dan notasi ilmiah yang sesuai dengan tabel yang diberikan:

1. Panjang:

- 2 cm = 2 x 10^4 nm (karena 1 cm = 10^4 nm)

- 20 in = 20 x 2.54 cm = 50.8 cm

- 72 km/jam = 20 m/s (karena 1 km/jam = 1000/3600 m/s)

2. Massa:

- 15 ons = 15 x 28.35 g = 425.25 g

- 20 mg = 20 x 10^3 ug (karena 1 mg = 10^3 ug)

- 3 kuintal = 3 x 10^4 g

3. Volume:

- 2 L = 2000 cm³ (karena 1 L = 1000 cm³)

- 25 cc = 25 mL

- 3 km/jam = 300 cm/s (karena 1 km/jam = 1000/3600 m/s)

4. Kecepatan:

- 15 m/s = 54 km/jam (karena 1 km/jam = 1000/3600 m/s)

- 3,6 km/jam = 36 cm/s

5. Massa Jenis:

- 800 kg/m³ = 0.8 g/cm³ (karena 1 kg/m³ = 10^-3 g/cm³)

- 1.2 g/cm³ = 1200 kg/m³

Tsunamis can also take the form if a very fast tide or bore, depending on the shape of the sea floor. Tsunamis have tremendous force because of the great volume of water affected and the speed at which they travel. Just a cubic yard of water , for example , weights about , one ton.
